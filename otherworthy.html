Rainbow Mosque

The Rainbow Mosque, also known as Masjid Al-Islamia, is a mosque located in Santa Catalina Island, Zamboanga City, Philippines. It is known for its vibrant exterior, adorned with a kaleidoscope of colors like red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet. The use of these colors is said to symbolize peace and unity.

Dennis Coffee Garden

This beloved cafe offers a cozy ambiance and a taste of local culture. Savor their famous "Kahawa Sug" coffee and explore delicious Tausug cuisine featuring fresh ingredients and vibrant flavors. With multiple locations and great reviews, it's a must-visit in Zamboanga City!

Merloquet Falls

Located about two hours away from the city center, near the boundary with Zamboanga Sibugay in the north and Zamboanga del Norte in the west, the magnificent two-tiered Merloquet Falls is regarded as one of the most beautiful not only in the region but in the country.
